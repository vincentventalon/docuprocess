# Competitive Intelligence Data Sources

## Review Platforms

### G2 (g2.com)
- **Best For**: B2B software reviews, category research
- **Data Available**: Ratings, reviews, feature comparisons, market presence
- **How to Use**:
  - Search "[category]" to find category page
  - Look at "Grid" for market leaders vs. niche players
  - Check individual profiles for detailed reviews
- **URL Pattern**: `g2.com/categories/[category-name]`

### Capterra (capterra.com)
- **Best For**: SMB software discovery
- **Data Available**: Ratings, pricing info, feature lists, screenshots
- **How to Use**:
  - Browse categories or search
  - Filter by features, pricing, company size
- **URL Pattern**: `capterra.com/[category]/software`

### TrustRadius
- **Best For**: Enterprise B2B software, detailed reviews
- **Data Available**: In-depth reviews, TrustMaps, feature ratings
- **How to Use**: Search for product categories

### GetApp
- **Best For**: Business app discovery
- **Data Available**: Ratings, feature lists, pricing
- **Note**: Owned by Gartner (same as Capterra)

## Product Discovery

### Product Hunt (producthunt.com)
- **Best For**: New product launches, emerging competitors
- **Data Available**: Launch info, upvotes, comments, maker info
- **How to Use**:
  - Search for category keywords
  - Filter by time to find recent launches
  - Check "Collections" for curated lists

### AlternativeTo (alternativeto.net)
- **Best For**: Finding similar products
- **Data Available**: Alternatives list, tags, platforms
- **How to Use**: Search known competitor to find alternatives

### SaaSHub (saashub.com)
- **Best For**: SaaS directory
- **Data Available**: Categories, alternatives, basic info
- **How to Use**: Browse categories or search

### BetaList (betalist.com)
- **Best For**: Early-stage startups
- **Data Available**: Pre-launch products, beta signups

---

## Company Intelligence

### Crunchbase (crunchbase.com)
- **Best For**: Funding info, company details
- **Data Available**: Funding rounds, investors, team size, acquisitions
- **Limitation**: Some data requires paid access

### LinkedIn
- **Best For**: Team size, hiring activity
- **Data Available**: Employee count, job openings, company updates
- **How to Use**: Check company pages for employee count

### BuiltWith (builtwith.com)
- **Best For**: Technology stack analysis
- **Data Available**: Tech stack, market share data
- **How to Use**: Enter competitor URL to see their stack

### SimilarWeb
- **Best For**: Traffic estimates
- **Data Available**: Monthly visits, traffic sources, geography
- **Limitation**: Accuracy varies, better for larger sites

## Search-Based Discovery

### Google Search Operators

```
"[category] software" - Basic category search
"[category] tools for [audience]" - Audience-specific
"best [category] software 2024" - Listicles
"[competitor] alternatives" - Find alternatives
"[competitor] vs" - Find comparisons
site:g2.com "[category]" - G2 results only
site:reddit.com "[category] software" - Reddit discussions
```

### Reddit
- **Best For**: Authentic user opinions, recommendations
- **Subreddits**: r/SaaS, r/startups, r/Entrepreneur, niche-specific
- **How to Use**: Search for "[category] recommendations" or "[problem] solution"

### Quora
- **Best For**: Question-based discovery
- **How to Use**: Search "[problem]" to find recommended solutions

## Industry Reports

### Free Resources

- **VC firm blogs**: Market analysis and trends
- **Industry newsletters**: Curated market updates

### Paid Resources
- **Gartner**: Magic Quadrants (expensive)
- **Forrester**: Wave reports (expensive)
- **CB Insights**: Market maps and analysis

## Social Signals

### Twitter/X
- **Best For**: Brand awareness, customer sentiment
- **How to Use**: Search brand mentions, check follower counts

### LinkedIn Company Pages
- **Best For**: Employee count, B2B presence
- **Data**: Team size, job postings, company updates

### YouTube
- **Best For**: Demo videos, tutorials, marketing approach
- **How to Use**: Search competitor name + "demo" or "tutorial"

## Usage Tips

1. **Start broad, then narrow**: Begin with G2/Capterra categories, then deep dive
2. **Cross-reference data**: Verify pricing/features across multiple sources
3. **Check recency**: Ensure information is current (especially pricing)
4. **Note data quality**: Some sources have better coverage for certain markets
5. **Save URLs**: Document where you found each piece of information
